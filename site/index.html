Our Methodology

How we collect, process, and analyze wastewater samples to detect SARS-CoV-2

Sample Collection

24-hour composite samples collected from wastewater treatment plants using automated samplers

RNA Extraction

Viral RNA concentrated and extracted using polyethylene glycol precipitation and magnetic bead methods

RT-qPCR Analysis

Quantitative PCR targeting N1 and N2 genes with normalization to pepper mild mottle virus
